Solid, the next decentralized web

Solid is the a world wide web for data. Lead by Sir Tim Berners-Lee, the inventor of the original world wide web, Solid lets anyone host their own data as linked data wherever they want. O can help your organization dive into the Solid web.

Supply chain and carbon tracking

Linked data is perfect for supply chains. It allows every company in a supply chain to store their own data but still link to the world outside their database. To solve hard problems like tracking CO2 emissions for every supplier, you need an integrated linked data supply chain ecosystem. O can help you change your supply chain tech stack into a linked supply chain tech stack.

Digital Credentials and Decentralized Identity

Identity verification needs to be stardardized. With more services being added to our digital world each day, users have thousands of disconnected ways of identifying themselves. Verifiable Credentials and Solid Authentication provides a common method of identity verification. O can help your organization standardize around w3c endorsed open identity specifications.

Decentralized Apps
